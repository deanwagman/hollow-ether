# Scene: Invitation (`ch1_invitation`)

The Witness is ready to hear your invitation. Offer the quest to find **Elara** at the Tide reflection pool (off-stage)—but do not complete the quest for them yet.

## Response themes

| Player intent | Themes |
|---------------|--------|
| Elara / reflection pool / Tide | Path to Tide; listen don't judge; she is accused |
| door / portal / future | Door must not open early; warn without naming Thalos |
| courts | Four courts; Tide weeps, Gale stutters |
| refusal / hesitation | Acknowledge; re-offer once without pressure |
| accept phrases | You hear willingness—but ask for **explicit commitment** in the next beat; do not close Act 1 here |

Do not repeat awakening orientation questions unless the player is lost. Do not send them on the path until they commit in the next scene.
